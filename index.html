Introduction

Penang, known as the "Pearl of the Orient," is a stunning island that combines rich culture, beautiful beaches, and delicious food. Its capital, George Town, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with colonial architecture, vibrant street art, and diverse cultures.

Whether you're exploring its lush natural landscapes, relaxing on pristine beaches, or experiencing its lively local culture, Penang promises an unforgettable adventure.
